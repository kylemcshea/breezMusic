Biography

Fabricio Almeida, known as Breez, is a born and raised Dj in Miami, Florida. Starting from the age of 12 he began to create his persona of Djing through his private events and club gigs. Performing with artists like Meduza, Carnage, Cash Cash, Noizu, and Dombresky, the young artist is just beginning his journey.
